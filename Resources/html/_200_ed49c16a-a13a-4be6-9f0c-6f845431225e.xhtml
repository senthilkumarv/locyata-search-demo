Task 1: Disable LAN Connections to the Core
This lab activity requires that your instructor make changes to the core configuration. Verify with your instructor that the core configuration has been updated.
In this task, you will shut down the LAN connections from your workgroup to the core. You will also verify the configuration settings on your workgroup switch and router.
