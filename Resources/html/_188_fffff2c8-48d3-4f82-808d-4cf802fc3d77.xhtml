Lab 7-1: Enabling Dynamic Routing to the Main Office
Introduction
Complete this lab activity to practice what you learned in the related module.
Activity Objective
In this activity, you will enable the use of the dynamic routing protocol RIP. After completing this activity, you will be able to meet these objectives:
  • Configure RIP on your workgroup router
  • Verify that RIP is operating
  • Remove the now unnecessary static route to an adjacent network
